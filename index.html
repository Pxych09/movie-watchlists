Guidelines
Welcome to Our Movie Watchlists

A shared space for everyone to log, rate, and discuss movies they've watched. Post your latest watch, leave comments on others' picks, and track your watchlist all in one place.

Getting Started
1
Log In
Use your username and password to access the app. Your session is saved so you won't need to log in every time.
2
Open Tools
Click Show Tools (or toggle it in the menu) to reveal the post form, watchlists, and sub-genre manager on the left sidebar.
3
Create Your First Post
Fill in the movie name, genre, rating, duration, and date watched. Add a caption to share your thoughts, then hit Post Movie.
Features at a Glance
Movie Feed
Browse all posts from every user. Search by movie title, genre, sub-genre, or username using the search bar.
Dashboard
At-a-glance stats: total posts, hours watched, average rating, genre breakdown, monthly trends, and streaks.
To-Do Watchlists
Save movies you plan to watch. Draft items, save them, and use Spin the Wheel to randomly pick your next movie.
Sub-Genres
Add custom sub-genre tags (e.g. "Slasher", "Space Opera") that can be attached to any post for richer filtering.
Comments
React to anyone's post with a comment. You can delete your own comments anytime.
Notifications
Get notified when someone comments on your post. Unread notifications are highlighted — click to jump to the post.
Mini Series
A separate section for logging mini-series and TV shows, accessible via the nav bar.
Edit & Delete
You can edit or delete your own posts at any time using the icons at the top-right of each post card.
Tips & Tricks
  • Link a To-Do item to a new post by checking it in the Saved To-Do Watchlists panel before posting — it auto-fills the movie name and removes it from the list after posting.
  • Use the Spin the Wheel roulette to pick a random movie from your watchlist when you can't decide what to watch.
  • The Dashboard panels and Tools sidebar visibility are remembered between sessions — set them up once and they stay.
  • Switch between Count and Avg Rating tabs in the monthly chart to see different views of your watch history.
  • Search the feed to quickly find posts by a specific user, genre, or sub-genre tag.
  • Duration format examples: 2h 15m, 1h, 45m. These are used to calculate total hours watched in the Dashboard.
